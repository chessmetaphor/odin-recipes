☼ Brown Sugar Milk Tea ☼

Description

Two servings of delicious milk tea with tapioca pearls. Perfect for a mid-afternoon snack.

Ingredients

  • Milk Tea
    • 40 g brown sugar
    • 30 ml water
    • 600 ml fresh milk
  • Tapioca Pearls
    • 100 g tapioca starch
    • 60 ml water
    • 50 g brown sugar

Steps

  • The Pearls

    1. Add the water and brown sugar to a boil in a pot. Add half the tapioca starch and mix well. Take off the heat and add the rest.
    2. Cut 2-3 cm pieces of the doughy mixture and roll them all into balls.
    3. Boil the tapioca pearls on high heat for 6-8 minutes. Remove them and set them aside in ice to cool down.

  • The Drink

    1. Add the water and sugar to a pot and bring to a boil. Wait until the mixture turns brown and thick. Turn the heat low and simmer the pearls in the liquid for 5 minutes.
    2. Remove from heat and let the mixture cool down and thicken up.
    3. Drizzle the newly made syrup around a glass and dump the tapioca pearls in. Add the milk to the glass.
