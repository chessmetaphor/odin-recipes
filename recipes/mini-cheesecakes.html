♡ Mini Strawberry Cheesecakes ♡

Description

This recipe will help you make twelve small, circular cheesecakes with strawberry topping and cream cheese filling. It is fairly easy, but takes a while to make.

Ingredients

  • Crust:

    • 1 cup Graham crackers
    • 45 g melted butter
    • 1 tbsp granulated sugar

  • Filling:

    • 340 g cream cheese
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1/3 cup sour cream
    • 2 eggs
    • 1/2 tsp Kosher salt

  • Topping:

    • 230 g strawberries
    • 1/4 cup sugar
    • 1 1/2 tsp lemon juice
    • 1 tsp cornstarch

Steps

  • Main Instructions

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
    2. Grind the graham crackers up with a food processor into crumbs.
    3. Add the crumbs and melted butter into a mixing bowl and stir.
    4. Scoop 1 tsp worth of crumbs into each mold in your muffin pan.
    5. Bake for 5-8 minutes, then let the crust cool down. Lower the oven temperature to 280°F.
    6. Add cream cheese, sour cream, and sugar to a mixing bowl and beat with an electric hand mixer.
    7. Whisk the eggs in a separate bowl, then add them to the mixing bowl. Add the Kosher salt and vanilla extract as well, then beat the mixture again until it is smooth.
    8. Pour the mixture on top of all of the crusts in the muffin pan. Every mold should be almost full.
    9. Bake again at 280°F for about 15 min, then turn off the heat and let it sit in the oven for 5 more minutes.
    10. Open the oven door halfway, and let the cheesecake cool in there for 1 to 2 hours.
    11. Cover the cheesecakes with plastic wrap, and let them chill in the fridge for 5 hours before drizzling the topping on them.

  • Strawberry Topping

    1. Add halved strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice to a saucepan and mix them over medium-high heat.
    2. Add the cornstarch with water. Mix constantly.
    3. Decrease the heat to medium-low, then simmer for 20 minutes. Stir occasionally.
    4. Let the topping cool before drizzling the cheesecakes with it.
